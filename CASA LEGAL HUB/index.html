About Us

Casa Legal Hub, established in 2022, is a premier law firm dedicated to providing professional legal services with a focus on mediation and alternative dispute resolution.

Through our partnerships with experienced legal practitioners well-versed in Rwanda's legal system, we ensure the delivery of best practices and trusted legal assistance. Our firm excels in corporate and business law while offering high-quality mediation services both within and outside the court system.

Our Vision

Casa Legal Hub envisions becoming one of the leading law firms in alternative dispute resolution in East Africa by 2035 and across the entire African continent by 2050.
